<p><strong>The Double Sheet Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unilateral Contact</li><li>No Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Double Sheet Sensor market consists of two primary types: Unilateral Contact and No Contact sensors. Unilateral Contact sensors utilize mechanical methods to detect the thickness of two stacked sheets by evaluating pressure changes when they come into contact. In contrast, No Contact sensors employ advanced technologies, such as ultrasonic or electromagnetic fields, to sense the presence of two sheets without physical contact. Both types are crucial for ensuring product quality and preventing machine damage in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
