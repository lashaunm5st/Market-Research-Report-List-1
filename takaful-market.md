<p><p>Takaful operates through two main market types: Life/Family Takaful and General Takaful. Life/Family Takaful focuses on providing financial protection and savings benefits to families, covering events like death, disability, or critical illness. Conversely, General Takaful encompasses various non-life insurance products, including property and liability coverage, designed to protect against unforeseen risks. Both types adhere to Sharia principles, promoting mutual assistance and risk-sharing among participants, fostering a cooperative approach to financial security.</p></p>
