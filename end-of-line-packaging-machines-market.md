<p><p>End-of-line packaging machines are categorized into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic machines perform all packaging functions without human intervention, enhancing efficiency and consistency, making them suitable for high-volume production. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require some manual input, offering flexibility and lower initial costs.
