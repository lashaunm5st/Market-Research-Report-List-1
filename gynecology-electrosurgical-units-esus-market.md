<p><strong>The Gynecology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gynecology electrosurgical units (ESUs) are essential in minimally invasive surgical procedures, utilizing electrical currents to cut tissue or coagulate blood. The market is categorized into monopole ESUs, which operate using a single active electrode and grounding pad, ideal for cutting and coagulation. Bipolar ESUs use two electrodes at the surgical site, providing precise tissue handling and reduced thermal spread. Vessel sealing units are specialized devices that simultaneously cut and seal blood vessels, enhancing hemostasis and improving surgical outcomes.</p></p>
