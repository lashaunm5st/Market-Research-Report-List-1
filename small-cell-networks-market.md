<p><p>Small cell networks encompass various market types designed to enhance mobile coverage and capacity. **Femtocells** are small, low-power devices used in homes or small businesses, connecting to the operator's network via broadband. **Picocells** serve larger areas, like office buildings or shopping centers, providing improved coverage and capacity. **Microcells** are designed for medium-sized areas, such as urban environments, offering greater range and power than picocells. **Metrocells** cover dense urban spaces, addressing high demand in areas like city centers or stadiums, ensuring seamless connectivity.</p></p>
