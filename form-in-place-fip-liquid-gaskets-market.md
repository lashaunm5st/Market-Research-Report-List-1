<p><p>The Form-in-Place (FIP) liquid gaskets market is segmented into conductive and non-conductive types. Conductive FIP gaskets are designed to provide electrical conductivity, making them ideal for applications in electronics and electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding. Non-conductive FIP gaskets, on the other hand, offer isolation from electrical currents, suitable for sealing applications in environments where conductivity is not required. Both types ensure effective sealing, flexibility in design, and ease of application in various industries, including automotive and consumer electronics.</p></p>
