<p><ul><li>Single-Core Cryogenic Cables</li><li>Multi-Core Cryogenic Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryogenic cables market is primarily categorized into single-core and multi-core cryogenic cables. Single-core cryogenic cables consist of a single conductor, designed for specific applications requiring minimal electromagnetic interference and effective thermal insulation. In contrast, multi-core cryogenic cables contain multiple conductors within a single sheath, allowing for greater power distribution and flexibility in complex systems. Both types are crucial for operations in extreme low-temperature environments, commonly used in industries like aerospace, medical, and superconducting technologies.</p></p>
