<p><p>The venous infusion set market is categorized into two main types: with filter and without filter. Infusion sets with filters are designed to remove particulate matter and bacteria from the fluid before it enters the patient's bloodstream, enhancing safety and reducing infections. Conversely, sets without filters are simpler and typically used in scenarios where filtration is not critical. The choice between the two types depends on clinical requirements, patient conditions, and institutional protocols, aiming to optimize infusion therapy.</p></p>
