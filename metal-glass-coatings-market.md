<p><p>The Metal & Glass Coatings market can be categorized into three main types: solventborne, waterborne, and UV coatings. Solventborne coatings use organic solvents as carriers, providing excellent adhesion and durability but may have higher VOC emissions. Waterborne coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering lower VOCs and environmental benefits. UV coatings are cured using ultraviolet light, resulting in rapid drying and enhanced hardness. Each type caters to specific industry needs, balancing performance, environmental impact, and application efficiency.</p></p>
