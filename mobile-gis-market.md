<p><p>The Mobile GIS market is categorized into On-Promise and On-Cloud types. On-Promise solutions are deployed locally on an organization’s infrastructure, offering greater control, security, and customization, ideal for sensitive data management. In contrast, On-Cloud solutions leverage remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and accessibility from any location with internet access. This model typically reduces IT overhead and facilitates collaboration among users. Organizations choose between these options based on their specific operational needs, data sensitivity, and resource capabilities.</p></p>
