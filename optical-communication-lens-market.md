<p><p>The optical communication lens market is segmented based on wavelength types, primarily including 1310nm and 1550nm, alongside others like 405nm. The 1310nm wavelength is favored for short-distance communications due to its higher bandwidth capabilities and lower dispersion, making it ideal for data centers and metropolitan networks. In contrast, 1550nm is preferred for long-distance transmissions, owing to its reduced signal attenuation and extended range. Other wavelengths, such as 405nm, are gaining traction in niche applications like sensors and specialty imaging systems.</p></p>
