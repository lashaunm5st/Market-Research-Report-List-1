<p><ul><li>Transmitter IC</li><li>Receiver IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless charging integrated circuits (ICs) market is divided primarily into two types: transmitter ICs and receiver ICs. Transmitter ICs are responsible for converting electrical energy into electromagnetic fields that can be transmitted over short distances, enabling charging without physical connectors. In contrast, receiver ICs capture these electromagnetic fields and convert them back into electrical energy to charge devices. Together, these ICs facilitate efficient and seamless wireless charging solutions for various applications, including smartphones, wearables, and electric vehicles.</p></p>
