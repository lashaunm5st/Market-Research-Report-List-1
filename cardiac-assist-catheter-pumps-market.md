<p><p>Cardiac assist catheter pumps are vital in managing heart failure by supporting cardiac function. The Left Ventricular Assist Device (LVAD) focuses on enhancing blood flow from the left ventricle to the aorta, aiding patients with severe heart issues. Conversely, the Right Ventricular Assist Device (RVAD) targets the right ventricle, facilitating blood flow to the lungs. Both types are essential for treating patients awaiting transplants or managing chronic heart conditions, significantly improving their quality of life and survival rates.</p></p>
