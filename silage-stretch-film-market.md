<p><p>The silage stretch film market comprises various types based on material, primarily LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) and LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene). LLDPE is favored for its superior tensile strength and stretchability, providing excellent protection against moisture and air. LDPE, while less strong, offers good puncture resistance and is often used where flexibility is crucial. Other materials may include biodegradable options and high-density polyethylene (HDPE), catering to specific needs in agricultural practices. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing silage preservation.</p></p>
