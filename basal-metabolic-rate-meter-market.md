<p><p>The Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) meter market is segmented into direct and indirect measuring instruments. Direct measuring instruments assess BMR by directly measuring oxygen consumption and carbon dioxide production during rest. In contrast, indirect measuring instruments estimate BMR through calculations based on factors like age, weight, height, and sex, often using equations or algorithms. Both types serve healthcare and fitness industries, catering to personalized nutrition and metabolic health monitoring to optimize wellness strategies and weight management.</p></p>
