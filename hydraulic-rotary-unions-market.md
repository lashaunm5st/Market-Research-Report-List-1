<p><p>The hydraulic rotary unions market is categorized into two main types: multi-passage and single-passage. Multi-passage rotary unions allow multiple fluid paths within a single unit, enabling the simultaneous transfer of fluids, which is ideal for complex machinery requiring various media. In contrast, single-passage rotary unions facilitate the transfer of one fluid path, suitable for simpler systems. Both types serve crucial roles in applications such as robotics, manufacturing, and marine industries, enhancing efficiency and safety in fluid transfer.</p></p>
