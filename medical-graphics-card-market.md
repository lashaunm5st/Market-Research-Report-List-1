<p><ul><li>Single-head Graphics Card</li><li>Multi-head Graphics Card</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical graphics card market encompasses two primary types: single-head and multi-head graphics cards. Single-head graphics cards are designed for standard imaging applications, supporting one monitor and providing adequate performance for basic visualizations. In contrast, multi-head graphics cards cater to advanced medical imaging needs, enabling multiple displays simultaneously, which is essential for complex diagnostics and detailed analyses. The choice between these types often depends on the specific requirements of medical professionals and the intricacies of the imaging tasks involved.</p></p>
