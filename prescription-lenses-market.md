<p><p>The prescription lenses market features various types designed to address specific vision needs. Single vision spectacle lenses are tailored for correcting one field of vision, either near or distance, making them ideal for users with straightforward vision requirements. Progressive lenses, on the other hand, provide a seamless transition between multiple vision zones, accommodating those with presbyopia who need both near and far sight correction without the visible lines found in bifocals. Each type enhances visual clarity and comfort.</p></p>
