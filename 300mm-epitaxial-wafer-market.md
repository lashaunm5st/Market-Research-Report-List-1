<p><p>The 300mm epitaxial wafer market is categorized into P-Type and N-Type wafers, each serving distinct applications in semiconductor manufacturing. P-Type wafers are doped with elements that create “holes” for positive charge carriers, commonly used in power devices and analog applications. On the other hand, N-Type wafers contain dopants that introduce extra electrons, facilitating faster electron mobility, ideal for high-performance digital circuits. The choice between P-Type and N-Type wafers influences device performance and efficiency in various electronic applications.</p></p>
