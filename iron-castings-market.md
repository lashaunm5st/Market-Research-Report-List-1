<p><strong>The Iron Castings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gray iron</li><li>Ductile iron</li><li>Malleable iron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iron castings market comprises three main types: gray iron, ductile iron, and malleable iron. Gray iron is known for its excellent casting properties and is widely used in automotive and machinery components due to its good machinability. Ductile iron, with its enhanced strength and ductility, is ideal for applications requiring toughness, such as pipes and automotive parts. Malleable iron, created through a heat treatment process, offers good wear resistance and is used in hardware and automotive industries, blending strength with versatility.</p></p>
