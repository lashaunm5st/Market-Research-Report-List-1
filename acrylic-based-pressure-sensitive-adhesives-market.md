<p><p>Acrylic-based pressure-sensitive adhesives (PSAs) are categorized into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based acrylic adhesives offer strong bonding capabilities and are resistant to environmental factors, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, water-based acrylic adhesives are more environmentally friendly, with lower volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and easier cleanup. They find use in consumer products and packaging, where lower toxicity is preferred. Both types provide excellent adhesion, flexibility, and long-term performance across various industries.</p></p>
