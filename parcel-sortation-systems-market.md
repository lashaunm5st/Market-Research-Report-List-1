<p><p>The parcel sortation systems market includes two key types: horizontal cross strip sorters and vertical cross strip sorters. Horizontal cross strip sorters operate in a flat plane, facilitating fast and efficient sorting of parcels that move laterally across the conveyor system. In contrast, vertical cross strip sorters function in a vertical arrangement, allowing for a compact design that efficiently manages space while directing parcels to multiple destinations. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and speed in logistics and distribution centers.</p></p>
