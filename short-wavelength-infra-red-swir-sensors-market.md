<p><p>The Short Wavelength Infrared (SWIR) sensors market is primarily segmented into line-scan and area-scan types. Line-scan sensors capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for applications like industrial inspection and manufacturing where continuous movement is involved. In contrast, area-scan sensors capture entire frames simultaneously, suitable for applications requiring detailed imagery in static environments, such as surveillance and remote sensing. Each type serves unique use cases based on the dynamic needs of various industries.</p></p>
