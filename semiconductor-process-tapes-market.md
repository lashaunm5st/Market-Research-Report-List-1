<p><strong>The Semiconductor Process Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Tape</li><li>Non-UV Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor process tapes market comprises primarily two types: UV tape and non-UV tape. UV tape is designed for photolithography applications, where it is exposed to ultraviolet light for patterning processes, ensuring high precision and adhesion. Non-UV tape, on the other hand, is used in various stages of semiconductor manufacturing without UV exposure, offering versatility and temperature resistance. Both types are essential for protecting semiconductor wafers during production and ensuring optimal performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
