<p><ul><li>Dry Dressing</li><li>Wet Dressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-adherent absorbent dressing market is primarily segmented into dry and wet dressings. Dry dressings are designed to absorb exudate while maintaining a dry surface to promote healing and minimize infection risk, making them suitable for low exudate wounds. In contrast, wet dressings keep the wound moist, facilitating autolytic debridement and aiding in pain reduction, ideal for moderate to high exudate wounds. Both types enhance patient comfort and wound healing, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
