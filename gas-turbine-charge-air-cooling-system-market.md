<p><p>The gas turbine charge air cooling system market includes three main types: compression refrigeration, absorption refrigeration, and regenerative refrigeration. Compression refrigeration utilizes mechanical compressors to cool air, offering high efficiency and reliability. Absorption refrigeration relies on heat sources for cooling, making it suitable for waste heat recovery applications. Regenerative refrigeration enhances efficiency by reusing waste heat from the cooling process, optimizing energy usage. Each type serves distinct needs in enhancing gas turbine performance and overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
