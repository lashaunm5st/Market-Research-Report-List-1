<p><p>The offshore wind turbines market is segmented into shallow water, transitional water, and deep water applications. Transitional water turbines bridge the gap between shallow and deep water, typically ranging from 30 to 60 meters, utilizing floating foundations. Deep water applications, over 60 meters deep, require advanced floating turbine technologies that allow access to stronger wind resources, driving higher energy production despite increased installation complexities and costs.</p></p>
