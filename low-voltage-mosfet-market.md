<p><p>The Low Voltage MOSFET market is primarily segmented into N-Channel and P-Channel devices. N-Channel MOSFETs are more commonly used due to their superior electron mobility, resulting in higher efficiency and faster switching speeds, making them ideal for power management and digital circuits. P-Channel MOSFETs, while less efficient, are crucial in load switching applications and for simplifying high-side switch configurations.
