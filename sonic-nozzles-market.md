<p><p>The Sonic Nozzles market can be categorized into two main types: Pressurized Systems and Vacuum Systems. Pressurized Systems operate by utilizing a high-pressure gas source to achieve sonic flow through the nozzle, enabling precise flow measurement and control in various industrial applications. Vacuum Systems, on the other hand, create a low-pressure environment that allows for the expansion of gases, effectively measuring flow rates at lower pressure conditions. Both systems are essential for applications requiring high accuracy and reliability in fluid dynamics.</p></p>
