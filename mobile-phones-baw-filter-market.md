<p><ul><li>FBAR BAW Filter</li><li>SMR BAW Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile phone BAW filter market is primarily divided into two types: FBAR (Film Bulk Acoustic Resonator) and SMR (Solidly Mounted Resonator) BAW filters. FBAR filters utilize a thin film technology to achieve high frequency and performance, making them suitable for modern, compact devices. In contrast, SMR filters are designed for robustness and stability, catering to a broader range of applications. Both types are essential for enhancing signal quality and minimizing interference in mobile communications.</p></p>
