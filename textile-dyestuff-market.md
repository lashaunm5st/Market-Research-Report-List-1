<p><strong>The Textile Dyestuff Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disperse Dyes</li><li>Reactive Dyes</li><li>Sulfur Dyes</li><li>Vat Dyes</li><li>Acid Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The textile dyestuff market comprises several key types, each with specific applications. Disperse dyes are typically used for dyeing synthetic fibers, offering vibrant colors. Reactive dyes bond chemically with fibers, ideal for cotton and other cellulosic textiles. Sulfur dyes provide excellent fastness and are suited for dark shades, primarily on cotton. Vat dyes are known for their high lightfastness and are used on cellulosic fibers through a reduction process. Acid dyes are primarily utilized for protein fibers like wool and silk, providing bright hues.</p></p>
