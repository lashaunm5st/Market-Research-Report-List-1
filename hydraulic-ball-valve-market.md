<p><p>A hydraulic ball valve is a type of valve that uses a spherical disc, or "ball," to control the flow of hydraulic fluid. The ball has a hole through the center, which aligns with the pipe flow when the valve is open, allowing fluid to pass through. When turned 90 degrees, the ball blocks the flow, effectively shutting off the system. Known for their durability and efficiency, hydraulic ball valves are commonly used in various applications, including industrial processes, oil and gas, and water management.</p><p>The Hydraulic Ball Valve Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period. Check valves prevent backflow, ensuring fluid flows in one direction, crucial for maintaining system integrity. Directional valves control the route of fluid flow, enabling precise management of hydraulic systems for various applications. Other valves may include safety valves or pressure relief valves, each serving specialized roles in fluid control. Together, these components enhance the efficiency and reliability of hydraulic systems across industries.</p></p>
