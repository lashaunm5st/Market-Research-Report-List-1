<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The luxury passenger car tire market encompasses two primary types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature plies that crisscross at angles, providing a stiffer sidewall and a softer ride, making them suitable for classic and vintage luxury cars. In contrast, radial tires have plies arranged radially from the center, offering improved fuel efficiency, better handling, and enhanced tread lifespan, which are critical for high-performance modern luxury vehicles. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements and consumer preferences.</p></p>
