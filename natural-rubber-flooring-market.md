<p><p>Natural rubber flooring is categorized into Ordinary Rubber and Modified Rubber markets. Ordinary Rubber flooring is made from pure natural rubber, offering excellent elasticity, durability, and eco-friendliness, ideal for residential and commercial spaces. Modified Rubber flooring incorporates additives or synthetic blends to enhance properties like abrasion resistance, UV stability, or chemical resistance, making it suitable for high-traffic or industrial areas. Both types provide slip resistance, comfort, and sustainability, catering to diverse applications based on performance needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
