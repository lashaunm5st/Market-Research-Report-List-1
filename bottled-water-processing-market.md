<p><p>The bottled water processing market consists of various equipment types essential for production. Filters are used for purifying water by removing impurities. Blow molders are utilized for shaping plastic bottles from preforms, ensuring lightweight and strong packaging. Shrink wrappers provide secure packaging for pallets or individual bottles, enhancing protection during transport. Additional equipment includes conveyors and labeling machines.</p></p>
