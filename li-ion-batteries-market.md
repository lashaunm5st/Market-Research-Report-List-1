<p><p>The Li-ion battery market consists of several types, each with distinct characteristics. Lithium Cobalt Oxide (LCO) offers high energy density, making it suitable for smartphones. Lithium Nickel Cobalt Aluminum Oxide (NCA) balances energy density and stability, while Lithium Manganese Oxide (LMO) is favored for power tools due to its high thermal stability. Lithium Titanate (LTO) excels in rapid charging, and Lithium Nickel Manganese Cobalt (NMC) provides a blend of performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
