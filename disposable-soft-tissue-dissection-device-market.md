<p><p>The Disposable Soft Tissue Dissection Device market is segmented into bipolar and monopolar devices, each serving distinct surgical needs. Bipolar devices utilize two electrodes to control current flow, minimizing thermal spread and enhancing precision, making them ideal for delicate procedures. In contrast, monopolar devices use a single electrode, generating a high-frequency current that facilitates cutting and coagulation across broader tissue areas. Both types are favored for their efficiency and safety in surgical applications, increasing their demand in various medical settings.</p></p>
