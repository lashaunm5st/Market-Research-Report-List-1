<p><p>Conductive Polymer Aluminum Solid Electrolytic Capacitors are available in two primary market types: Surface Mount Type and Through-Hole Type. Surface Mount Type capacitors are designed for mounting directly on the surface of printed circuit boards (PCBs), offering compact sizes and improved performance for high-frequency applications. In contrast, Through-Hole Type capacitors are inserted into drilled holes in the PCB, providing enhanced stability and mechanical strength, making them suitable for applications where reliability is paramount, such as automotive and industrial electronics.</p></p>
