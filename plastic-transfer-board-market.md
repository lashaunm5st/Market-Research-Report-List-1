<p><p>The plastic transfer board market can be categorized into two main types: boards with rollers and boards without rollers. Boards with rollers facilitate smoother and easier transfers for patients by reducing friction, enhancing mobility, and allowing caregivers to position individuals with less physical effort. In contrast, boards without rollers rely on a flat surface for transfers, which may require more manual handling but can be more cost-effective and durable. Both types serve crucial roles in patient mobility and caregiver assistance.</p></p>
