<p><p>The missile guidance system market consists of several key types. Command guidance systems use external signals for navigation, while homing guidance systems autonomously track targets through sensors. Beam rider guidance systems follow a beam emitted from the launch platform for precision targeting. Inertial and GPS guidance systems rely on internal sensors and satellite data to determine the missile's position, enhancing accuracy over long distances. Each system serves specific military applications, impacting market dynamics and technological advancements.</p></p>
