Hydrophobic coating technology involves applying a thin layer of specialized compounds to the glass surface, enhancing its water-repellent properties, which reduces visibility issues during rain. On the other hand, nanofilm technology utilizes advanced nano-coatings that create a durable, transparent layer, further improving water resistance and durability. Both technologies significantly enhance driving safety and visibility, contributing to the growing demand for advanced automotive glass solutions.</p></p>
