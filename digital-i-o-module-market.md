<p><p>The Digital I/O Module market is divided into two main types: Wired and Wireless Digital I/O Modules. Wired Digital I/O Modules connect directly to control systems through cables, providing reliable communication and lower latency, making them ideal for industrial settings. In contrast, Wireless Digital I/O Modules utilize radio frequency or other wireless technologies to transmit data, offering greater flexibility and easier installation, especially in hard-to-reach areas. Both types cater to diverse applications in automation, monitoring, and data acquisition.</p></p>
