<p><p>The pharmaceutical warehousing market comprises two primary types: non-cold chain and cold chain warehouses. Non-cold chain warehouses are designed for storing stable medications that do not require temperature control, offering a cost-effective solution for general pharmaceuticals. In contrast, cold chain warehouses maintain specific temperature conditions essential for the storage of temperature-sensitive products, such as vaccines and biologics, ensuring their efficacy and safety. Together, these warehouses support the comprehensive distribution needs of the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
