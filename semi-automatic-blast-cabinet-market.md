<p><strong>The Semi-Automatic Blast Cabinet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Blast Cabinet</li><li>Suction Blast Cabinet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic blast cabinet market is divided into pressure blast cabinets and suction blast cabinets. Pressure blast cabinets use compressed air to propel abrasive materials forward at high speeds, allowing for efficient and aggressive surface cleaning or finishing. Conversely, suction blast cabinets utilize a vacuum system to draw abrasives through a nozzle, providing a gentler approach suitable for delicate surfaces. Both types are essential in various industries for enhancing product quality and surface preparation, catering to different operational needs and materials.</p></p>
