<p><p>The **Automotive Dynamic Steering System** market includes **Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)** and **Electric Power Steering (EPS)** systems. HPS uses hydraulic pressure generated by a pump to assist steering, offering a traditional, robust feel but with higher energy consumption. EPS relies on an electric motor for assistance, providing better fuel efficiency, adaptability, and integration with advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS). EPS is increasingly favored for its lightweight design and eco-friendly performance, while HPS remains prevalent in heavy-duty vehicles due to its durability.</p></p>
