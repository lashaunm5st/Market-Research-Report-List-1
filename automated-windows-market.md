<p><p>Automated windows are categorized into three main market types: mechanical, electric, and hybrid. Mechanical types rely on manual mechanisms for operation, often using gears or pulleys, providing a cost-effective solution for basic automation. Electric types utilize electric motors for precise control and ease of use, enabling remote operation and integration with smart home systems. Hybrid types combine both mechanical and electric elements, offering flexibility and efficiency, catering to various user needs and preferences in enhancing comfort and energy efficiency.</p></p>
