<p><p>The 18650 battery pack market is segmented into series and parallel configurations. A series battery pack connects multiple cells in a sequence, increasing voltage while maintaining the same capacity, making it ideal for applications needing higher voltage. In contrast, a parallel battery pack combines cells to enhance overall capacity while maintaining voltage, benefiting devices requiring extended runtime. Each configuration addresses specific energy needs, influencing their use in electric vehicles, portable electronics, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
