<p><p>The autotransfusion devices and consumables market consists of various types, primarily categorized into devices and consumables. Devices include on-pump transfusion systems, which operate alongside heart-lung machines during surgeries, enabling immediate recycling of blood, and off-pump transfusion systems that collect and reinfuse blood without the need for cardiopulmonary bypass. Consumables encompass blood collection and processing kits, ensuring effective blood handling and safety. These innovations enhance surgical outcomes, minimize waste, and promote patient safety in transfusion practices.</p></p>
