<p><p>The immunoassay instruments/analyzers market encompasses various types that facilitate the detection of specific proteins or antibodies. Chemiluminescence immunoassay uses light emission for measurement, while immunofluorescence analyzers employ fluorescent labels. Radioimmunoassay utilizes radioisotopes for sensitivity, and enzyme-linked immunoassay (ELISA) uses enzyme reactions to indicate the presence of antigens. Additionally, the consumables market includes reagents, kits, and other materials essential for conducting these assays, contributing significantly to the overall functionality and efficiency of immunodiagnostic testing.</p></p>
