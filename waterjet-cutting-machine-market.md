<p><ul><li>Pure Waterjet Machines</li><li>Abrasive Waterjet Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet cutting machine market comprises two primary types: pure waterjet machines and abrasive waterjet machines. Pure waterjet machines utilize high-pressure streams of water to cut soft materials like foam, rubber, and textiles, providing a clean and precise cut. In contrast, abrasive waterjet machines incorporate abrasive materials mixed with water to effectively cut harder substances such as metals and stone. Both types offer advantages in accuracy and versatility, catering to a wide range of industries, including manufacturing and construction.</p></p>
