<p><p>The Filler Alumina market is categorized into two primary types: Micron Grade and Millimeter Grade. Micron Grade alumina comprises very fine particles, typically used in applications like paints, coatings, and plastics, where high surface area and fine dispersion are essential for enhanced performance. Millimeter Grade alumina, with larger particle sizes, is utilized in applications requiring bulk and structural reinforcement, such as ceramics and composites. Each grade serves distinct industrial needs, driving innovation and efficiency across various sectors.</p></p>
