<p><p>The anti-aging additives for polymer materials market is primarily comprised of three types: Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS), UV Absorbers, and Antioxidants. HALS are effective in preventing polymer degradation caused by UV exposure, enhancing longevity. UV Absorbers work by absorbing harmful ultraviolet radiation, thereby protecting the polymer matrix. Antioxidants mitigate oxidative degradation by neutralizing free radicals, preserving physical properties. Together, these additives significantly extend the lifespan and performance of various polymer products used in diverse applications.</p></p>
