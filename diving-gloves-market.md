<p><strong>The Diving Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Wet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diving gloves market is primarily divided into two types: dry and wet gloves. Dry gloves are designed to keep hands completely insulated from water, making them ideal for extreme cold and deep-sea diving. They typically feature a waterproof seal and are used in colder environments. In contrast, wet gloves are made from neoprene or similar materials, allowing some water to enter while providing insulation and grip. They are suitable for warmer waters and recreational diving, offering flexibility and comfort.</p></p>
