<p><p>The transient suppressor (TVS) diode market is segmented into uni-polar and bi-polar types. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed for unidirectional clamping of voltage spikes, ideal for protecting circuits from overvoltage in a single direction. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes can handle bidirectional surges, making them suitable for applications where voltage can swing in either direction. This diversity allows users to select the appropriate device based on their specific circuit protection needs, influencing market demand and product development.</p></p>
