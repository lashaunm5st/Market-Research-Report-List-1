<p><p>Cardanol, derived from cashew nut shell liquid, is available in two main market types: Single Distilled and Double Distilled. Single Distilled Cardanol undergoes one purification process, retaining some impurities, making it cost-effective and suitable for industrial applications like coatings and resins. Double Distilled Cardanol is refined twice, resulting in higher purity and superior quality, ideal for advanced applications such as high-performance polymers and specialty chemicals. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing cost and quality based on end-use requirements.</p></p>
