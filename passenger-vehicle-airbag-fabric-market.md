<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Airbag Fabric Market includes two primary types: Flat Airbag Fabric and OPW (One-Piece Woven) Airbag Fabric. Flat Airbag Fabric is made from woven nylon yarns, offering durability and flexibility, commonly used in side and curtain airbags. OPW Airbag Fabric is a seamless, one-piece woven design, providing superior strength and airtightness, ideal for frontal airbags. Both fabrics prioritize safety, lightweight properties, and resistance to high-impact forces, ensuring effective deployment and protection during collisions. Their demand is driven by advancements in vehicle safety standards.</p></p>
