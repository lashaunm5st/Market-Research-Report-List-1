<p><ul><li>Mono</li><li>Stereo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camera microphone market is primarily categorized into mono and stereo types. Mono microphones capture sound in a single channel, making them ideal for straightforward recording scenarios, such as vlogs or interviews, where focused audio clarity is essential. Stereo microphones, on the other hand, utilize two channels to create a more immersive sound experience, enhancing the depth and spatial quality of recordings. This makes them suitable for capturing live events, music, or environments where a realistic audio portrayal is desired.</p></p>
