<p><p>Autonomous shuttles are categorized by their automation levels. **L3 (Level 3)** shuttles offer conditional automation, requiring human intervention in complex scenarios. They operate autonomously in specific environments but need a driver as a backup. **L4 (Level 4)** shuttles are highly autonomous, functioning without human input in predefined areas or conditions, though they may have limitations in extreme situations. **L5 (Level 5)** shuttles are fully autonomous, capable of operating in any environment without human intervention, representing the pinnacle of self-driving technology.</p></p>
