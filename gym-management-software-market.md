<p><p>Gym management software is categorized primarily into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted online, allowing gym owners to access the system from anywhere with internet connectivity. This flexibility ensures automatic updates and scalability, making it a popular choice. On-premise software, however, is installed locally on a gym's servers, providing greater control and security over data but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and budget considerations for gym administrators.</p></p>
