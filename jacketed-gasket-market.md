<p><p>A jacketed gasket is a specialized sealing component used in industrial applications to prevent leakage between two surfaces, particularly in high-pressure or high-temperature environments. It consists of a metal core encased in a soft, compressible material like PTFE, graphite, or elastomers, offering enhanced sealing performance and durability. These gaskets are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, power generation, and pharmaceuticals due to their ability to withstand extreme conditions and corrosive media.</p><p>The Jacketed Gasket Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.1% during the forecast period. <p><p>The jacketed gasket market is categorized by material types: Semi-Metallic, Non-Metallic, and Metallic. Semi-Metallic gaskets combine metals with non-metals, offering durability and flexibility for moderate pressure and temperature applications. Non-Metallic gaskets, made from materials like rubber or graphite, are ideal for low-pressure, low-temperature environments, providing excellent chemical resistance. Metallic gaskets, constructed from solid metals, are designed for high-pressure, high-temperature systems, ensuring robust sealing in demanding industrial settings. Each type caters to specific operational needs, balancing performance, cost, and application requirements.</p></p>
