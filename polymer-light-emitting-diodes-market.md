<p><p>The Polymer Light Emitting Diodes (PLED) market is primarily categorized into two types: Passive Matrix and Active Matrix. Passive Matrix displays use a grid of conductors to control the pixels, making them simpler and cost-effective but less efficient for larger screens. In contrast, Active Matrix incorporates thin-film transistors for each pixel, enabling greater control and faster response times, making it suitable for high-resolution and larger displays. These distinctions influence applications ranging from small screens to advanced display technologies.</p></p>
